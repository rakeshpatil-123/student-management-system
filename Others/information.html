About the Department

The Information Technology Department at BIST is dedicated to equipping students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving world of IT. The department focuses on innovation, problem-solving, and real-world applications, ensuring that graduates are industry-ready.

Our esteemed Head of Department (HOD), Prof. Dr. Rashmi Singh , leads the department with his expertise in IT solutions, cybersecurity, and software development. With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Rashmi Singh fosters an environment of growth and collaboration.
