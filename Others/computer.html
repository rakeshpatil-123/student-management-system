About the Department

The Computer Science Department at BIST is committed to providing high-quality education in computer science and engineering. Our mission is to nurture problem solvers, innovators, and leaders who excel in the ever-changing technological landscape. With a focus on practical learning, industry collaborations, and cutting-edge research, we prepare students for the challenges of the digital era.

Led by our visionary Head of Department (HOD), Prof. Dr. Anjali Sharma, the department fosters an environment of creativity and collaboration. Prof. Sharma brings over 20 years of academic and industry experience to inspire the next generation of tech leaders.

Core Subjects

Operating Systems

Artificial Intelligence

Explore AI techniques like neural networks, machine learning, and deep learning.

Computer Networks

Dive into the principles of networking, protocols, and internet technologies.

Cybersecurity

Learn to secure systems and networks against emerging cyber threats.

Database Management

Master relational databases, SQL, and database design principles.
