About the Department

The Electrical Engineering Department at BIST is dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in the field of power systems, electrical machines, and control systems. With a focus on sustainability and cutting-edge technology, the department prepares students to address the challenges of the modern energy landscape.

Under the leadership of our esteemed Head of Department, Prof. Dr. Anita Deshmukh, the department emphasizes hands-on learning, research-driven insights, and collaboration with the energy industry.

Dr. Anita Deshmukh

Core Subjects

Power Systems

Study the generation, transmission, and distribution of electrical power.

Electrical Machines

Learn about the design and operation of transformers, motors, and generators.

Control Systems

Understand the principles of automation and feedback mechanisms.
