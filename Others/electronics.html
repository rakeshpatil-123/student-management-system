About the Department

The Electronics and Communication Engineering Department at BIST bridges the gap between innovation and practical application in the field of communication, signal processing, and embedded systems. Our vision is to produce engineers capable of designing advanced electronic systems for a connected future.

Under the guidance of our Head of Department, Prof. Dr. Rashmi Saxena, the department emphasizes cutting-edge research, industry collaboration, and hands-on learning through state-of-the-art laboratories and facilities.

Dr. Rashmi Saxena

Head of Electronics and Communication Engineering Department

Core Subjects

Digital Electronics

Understand the design and working of digital circuits and logic gates.

Signal Processing

Learn techniques to analyze and manipulate signals for communication systems.

Embedded Systems

Design and develop systems with microcontrollers and real-time processing.
