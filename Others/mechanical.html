About the Department

The Mechanical Engineering Department is committed to providing students with a strong foundation in design, manufacturing, and thermodynamics. The department fosters innovation and prepares students for a wide range of industries, including automotive, aerospace, and energy.

Led by Prof. Dr. Rashmi Saxena, an expert in thermal systems and fluid mechanics, the department emphasizes practical learning through hands-on projects and state-of-the-art labs.

Dr. Rashmi Saxena

Head of Mechanical Engineering Department

Core Subjects

Thermodynamics

Study the principles of energy, heat, and work in various systems.

Fluid Mechanics

Understand the behavior of fluids in motion and at rest.

CAD (Computer-Aided Design)

Learn to create precise designs and models using advanced tools.
