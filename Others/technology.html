Who We Are

Bansal Group of Institutions (BGI), established in 2000 in Bhopal, is a leading academic institution offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, management, and technology. With a mission to empower young minds through quality education and a vision to be recognized globally, BGI has consistently delivered on its promise to transform students into future leaders.

Our state-of-the-art campus provides a nurturing environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and collaboration. Equipped with advanced laboratories, well-stocked libraries, and expert faculty, BGI is committed to academic excellence and holistic development. From cultural events to technical fests, every activity is designed to enhance student life and prepare them for global challenges.
