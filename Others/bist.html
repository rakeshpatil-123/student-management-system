Welcome to BGI

Bansal Institute of Science and Technology (BIST), situated in Bhopal, is a premier educational institution dedicated to excellence in technical education. Established with the vision of empowering young minds, BIST has carved a niche in the realm of science, technology, and innovation.

The institute offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across disciplines such as engineering, computer applications, and management. The curriculum is meticulously designed to foster technical proficiency, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, equipping students for global challenges.

At BIST, we believe in holistic development. Our sprawling campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, advanced computing centers, a comprehensive library, and collaborative learning spaces. Beyond academics, students actively engage in cultural events, technical fests, and extracurricular activities, nurturing leadership and teamwork.

With a dedicated faculty comprising seasoned academicians and industry professionals, BIST ensures a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application. Our strong industry linkages facilitate internships and placements, empowering students to embark on successful careers in reputed organizations.

Join us at BIST to be part of a transformative journey where education meets innovation, and ambition meets opportunity. Together, let's shape the future and redefine excellence.
